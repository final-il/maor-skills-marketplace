# Architecture Decision Record (ADR) Template

```yaml
---
doc_type: adr
title: "ADR-NNN: [Decision Title]"
author: ""
date: YYYY-MM-DD
version: "1.0"
status: draft
audience: engineering
tags: []
---
```

## Structure

```markdown
# ADR-NNN: [Decision Title]

## Status
[Proposed | Accepted | Deprecated | Superseded by ADR-XXX]

## Context
What is the issue that we're seeing that is motivating this decision?
Include technical context, business constraints, and what has changed
that makes this decision necessary now.

## Decision
What is the change that we're proposing and/or doing?
Be specific — name technologies, patterns, and approaches.

## Alternatives Considered

### Alternative 1: [Name]
- **Description**: What this option looks like
- **Pros**: Benefits
- **Cons**: Drawbacks
- **Why not**: Reason for rejection

### Alternative 2: [Name]
...

## Consequences

### Positive
- What becomes easier or better

### Negative
- What becomes harder or worse
- New risks introduced

### Neutral
- Changes that are neither good nor bad but worth noting

## Action Items
| Action | Owner | Due Date |
|--------|-------|----------|
| ... | ... | ... |

## References
- Links to related ADRs, design docs, RFCs
```
